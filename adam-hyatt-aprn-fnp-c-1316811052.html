About Adam Hyatt, APRN, FNP-C

Zion Direct Care stands out by combining over a decade of Emergency Medicine experience with a modern, mobile, concierge-style approach that brings high-quality medical care directly to patientswhether at home, work, a hotel, or on the trail. Our practice eliminates the frustrations of traditional healthcare by offering transparent cash pricing, same-day access, zero insurance barriers, and unhurried visits focused entirely on the patients needs. Unlike standard urgent care or primary care clinics, we deliver a seamless blend of family and urgent care, IV therapy, medical weight management, peptide and regenerative medicine, and wellness optimization, giving patients a full spectrum of evidence-based care in one place. Your holistic, personalized approachrooted in safety, clinical expertise, and conveniencecreates a healthcare experience that is simpler, more accessible, and genuinely patient-centered.
